Introduction

My name is Paul Struble, and I am a software engineer and data scientist in the San Francisco Bay Area. I am currently an undergrad at UC Berkeley double majoring in Computer Science and Data Science. I have over 4 years of programming experience and have been involved in other areas of tech, namely PC hardware, for far longer. Most recently, I have been building skills for full stack web development, but my interests and skills remain diverse.
